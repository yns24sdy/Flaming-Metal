In 2025, ESP Guitars celebrates 50 years of crafting iconic instruments for metal and rock legends. Known for precision and artist collaborations (Metallica, Slayer), the brand will unveil limited editions and events worldwide. The legacy of heavy tone lives on.
Metal heavyweight Slipknot's guitarist Mick Thomson (iconic #7 mask) has ended his long-term partnership with Jackson and signed with ESP Guitars. As a key 50th-anniversary signing, ESP will develop custom signature models for Thomson, maintaining his brutal tonal legacy. This move strengthens ESP's presence in extreme metal.
2025 marks the 50th anniversary of Motörhead, the legendary band that redefined heavy music. Founded by Lemmy Kilmister in 1975, their raw power and iconic tracks like "Ace of Spades" cemented their legacy. Expect special reissues, tributes, and global events honoring their "everything louder than everything else" spirit.
Rock icon Ozzy Osbourne will reunite with Black Sabbath for one final performance in their hometown of Birmingham on July 5, 2025. This emotional homecoming concert marks the definitive end of the pioneering metal band's touring career, giving fans worldwide a last chance to experience their legendary sound live.

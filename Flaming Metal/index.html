BRIEF NEWS

In 2025, ESP Guitars celebrates 50 years of crafting iconic instruments for metal and rock legends. Known for precision and artist collaborations (Metallica, Slayer), the brand will unveil limited editions and events worldwide. The legacy of heavy tone lives on.
Metal heavyweight Slipknot's guitarist Mick Thomson (iconic #7 mask) has ended his long-term partnership with Jackson and signed with ESP Guitars. As a key 50th-anniversary signing, ESP will develop custom signature models for Thomson, maintaining his brutal tonal legacy. This move strengthens ESP's presence in extreme metal.
